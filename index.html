Deal structure

Every deal is custom-built.

We don't have a fixed price list. We negotiate the balance between our investment and your payment — case by case, deal by deal.

Model A

Revenue Share

We invest our resources into your launch — team, infrastructure, operations — and earn a success fee as a % of sales. You pay nothing upfront. We succeed only when you do.

Best for: companies ready to commit to Russia long-term and willing to share upside.
Model B

Service Fee

You pay a fixed monthly fee for our operational services — team, legal, accounting, sales ops. Predictable costs, full transparency.

Best for: companies that want full control over economics and prefer a vendor model.

Most deals are a combination of both.

The split between investment and service fee is a negotiation — from 100% revenue share (we invest everything) to 100% service fee (you pay for everything), and every ratio in between. The balance reflects risk appetite, market potential, and timeline.
